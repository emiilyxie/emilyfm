Music Mentors Club
At the beginning of my sophomore year in high school, I founded the Music Mentor’s club to connect K-8 music students and high school music students in a mentee-mentor relationship. The Music Mentor’s club plans to foster a musical awareness in younger children and to provide high schoolers with a valuable teaching experience.
